Mission!

Social Change

We decided we wanted to create a games that teaches player about how people are viewed while on different levels of the economy. When the player has a few coins they are the smaller man who is more worn down. As they collect more coins they become bigger and the smaller people disappear. The game is meant to be a rude awakening in a way.
